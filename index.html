03 — Process

My Workflow

01
Understanding the Idea

Before a single cut, I understand the purpose of the video.

  • Who is the audience?
  • What is the main message?
  • What emotion should the viewer feel?
02
Content Review & Story Selection

I go through all footage and build a rough story structure before editing starts.

  • Strong moments & key statements
  • Emotional & informative highlights
  • Logical story flow
03
Rough Cut

This stage is purely about story and pacing — no heavy effects yet.

  • Cut unnecessary pauses
  • Arrange clips logically
  • Ensure the message is clear
04
Sound Design & Music

Sound plays a huge role in engagement. I build a sonic layer that enhances without distracting.

  • Background music to support the mood
  • Subtle SFX for transitions
  • Emphasis sounds for key moments
05
Motion Graphics & Visual Elements

Once structure is solid, I layer in motion to visualise ideas and hold attention.

  • Text animations & UI-style graphics
  • Visual explanations
  • Subtitles and highlights
06
Colour & Final Polish

The finishing pass that makes everything look intentional and professional.

  • Colour balance & consistent grade
  • Smooth transitions
  • Final audio balancing & export
